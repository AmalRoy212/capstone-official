Welcome to Capstone Business Intelligence!

Capstone Business Intelligence is a global leader in business events and consulting, dedicated to providing tailored services for corporations, governments, associations, and high-net-worth individuals. We specialize in B2B events, intelligent demand generation, bespoke conferences, and consulting services that connect businesses with opportunities.

Information We Collect

  • • Personal Information: We may collect personal and business details, such as name, contact information, and company details, when you register, make an inquiry, or book services through our platform.
  • • Log Data: Our servers automatically collect data such as IP addresses, browser type, referral URLs, and activity logs. This data helps us analyze site usage and improve user experience.
  • • Location Data: We may collect location data from your device to personalize your experience. You can disable location sharing through your device settings.
  • • Analytics and Tracking: We use tools like Google Analytics and HubSpot to gather anonymous data for service improvements. Cookies and other tracking technologies may be used—please refer to our Cookies Policy for more details.
  • • Social Media Features: Interactions with features like "Like" or "Share" buttons on our website may collect certain data governed by the respective social media platform's policies.

Use of Information

  • • Communication: To respond to inquiries, register attendees, issue tickets, and share relevant content.
  • • Marketing: To provide updates, promotional materials, and tailored services related to our events.
  • • Compliance: To fulfill legal obligations and address disputes or claims.
  • • Service Improvement: To conduct audits, analyze trends, and enhance user experience.

Data Retention

We retain your information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Information Sharing

  • We do not share your personal or business information except as necessary to operate our business or with your consent. This may include: sharing with affiliates, service providers, or partners for business operations; publishing brief quotes, bios, or award nominations for promotional purposes; sharing publicly submitted documents or content with event attendees; complying with legal obligations or investigations of suspected fraud or abuse.

Protecting Your Information

We prioritize the security of your data through robust protection measures. However, public data submissions are beyond our control and may be accessed by third parties.
